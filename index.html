Frequently Asked Questions

What is UPF Detector?

UPF Detector is a free iOS app that helps you identify ultra-processed foods by scanning product barcodes. It uses the NOVA classification system to determine the processing level of food products.

How does it work?

Simply open the app and scan any food product's barcode. The app will instantly tell you whether the product is ultra-processed and provide detailed ingredient information.

Is the app free?

Yes! UPF Detector is completely free with no ads, no subscriptions, and no in-app purchases.
