Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 10, 2025

Introduction

UPF Detector ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data when you use our iOS application.

Information We Collect

UPF Detector collects minimal data to function:

  • Scan history (stored locally on your device)
  • Anonymous usage statistics to improve the app
  • Product barcode information when you scan items

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Display your scan history
  • Improve app functionality
  • Provide product information

Data Storage

All scan history is stored locally on your device. No personal information is transmitted to our servers.

Third-Party Services

We use the Open Food Facts database to provide product information. Their privacy policy can be found at https://world.openfoodfacts.org/legal.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: hello@upfdetector.com
